How to Reduce Power Consumption

  • Turn off appliances when not in use: Unplugging or turning off devices can prevent power wastage.
  • Use energy-efficient appliances: Look for energy-saving models with higher efficiency ratings.
  • Use timers or smart plugs: Set timers for appliances to run only when necessary, and use smart plugs to control them remotely.
  • Switch to LED lights: LED lights use less energy and have a longer lifespan compared to traditional bulbs.
  • Optimize appliance usage: Use appliances like air conditioners, refrigerators, and washing machines efficiently by avoiding overuse and setting appropriate temperatures.
  • Maintain your appliances: Regular cleaning and maintenance of appliances, like air conditioners and refrigerators, can help them run more efficiently.
